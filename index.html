Smart Energy Savings Tips

Discover practical ways to reduce your energy consumption and save money on your monthly bills

Optimize Temperature Settings

Set your thermostat to 78°F in summer and 68°F in winter. Each degree adjustment can save up to 3% on cooling/heating costs.

LED Lighting Upgrade

Replace incandescent bulbs with LED lights. They use 75% less energy and last 25 times longer, saving you money on replacements.

Manage Phantom Loads

Use smart power strips to eliminate standby power consumption from electronics, which can account for 10% of your electric bill.

Calculate Your Energy Savings

Use our interactive calculator to estimate how much you could save by implementing energy-efficient practices in your home.
